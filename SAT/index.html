SAT exam eligibility is specified by the official SAT conducting agency, the College Board. SAT is for admission to undergraduate programs, candidates need to appear for their SAT while in High school or have completed High school. There is no minimum or maximum age limit for candidates wanting to register for SAT exam. However, applicants belonging to the age group of 17-19 years have recorded the highest turnout for the SAT exams. SAT exam eligibility for African students is having a valid passport. Countries that majorly accept the SAT scores and results are the USA, Canada, the UK, Singapore, the Netherlands, Australia, and New Zealand among others.
